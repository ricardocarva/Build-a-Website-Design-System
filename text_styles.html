Text Styles Guide 101

In this Website, you will find some colors, fonts, and text styles that you may use on your next project. Use the navigation bar to explore!

H1: Main page heading

  • Font-weight: 700 (bold)
  • Font-size: 32px
  • Font-family: Roboto

H2: Subheading

  • Font-weight: 700 (bold)
  • Font-size: 24px
  • Font-family: Roboto

P: Paragraph text

  • Font-weight: 400
  • Font-size: 16px
  • Font-family: Roboto
